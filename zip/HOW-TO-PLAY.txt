This is a video game in PDF format.

It cannot be played using Adobe Acrobat Reader,
that renders pages too slowly.

Use instead another PDF reader, like Sumatra PDF: https://www.sumatrapdfreader.org
Full list of compatible PDF readers: https://github.com/Lucas-C/undying-dusk#compatible-pdf-readers

Game web page: https://lucas-c.itch.io/undying-dusk

Feeling stuck in the game? Ask for advice on Reddit: https://www.reddit.com/r/UndyingDuskPdfGame
